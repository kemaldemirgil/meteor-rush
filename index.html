Meteor Rush

        Press the following keys for the controls:
        Arrow keys = Movement
        Space bar = Shoot

        When the score reaches 20, level 2 starts
        When it reaches 40, level 3 starts
        When the score reaches 100, you win!
        Stars give you extra life
        Boosts give you faster laser shots
        Avoid hitting the meteors and letting them pass by

        GOOD LUCK!
